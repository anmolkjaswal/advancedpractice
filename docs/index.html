Our Story

I'm Amy and I'd love to be your photographer! I specialize in family and wedding photography. I've been into photography since I was 13 years old! I picked up my first DSLR at the age of 14, and started from there. Now 10 years later, I am doing what I love for a living. I shoot with a Canon 5d Mark IV, and mainly use Prime Lenses. My favorite types of photographs are ones filled with real emotion - crying, laughter, passion, I want to capture it all! Feel free to contact me at any time!
